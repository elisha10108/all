Menachem Begin Park is a park in south Tel Aviv-Yafo, named after Menachem Begin. The maintenance and operation of the park is done under the responsibility of Ganei Yehoshua, the manager of the Yarkon Park. The park is located north and east of Nahal Ayalon between the agricultural fields of Mikve Israel and the Argazim neighborhood. The park was designed by landscape architect Zvi Dekel and covers 1,000 dunams, but currently only 500 dunams of it are developed.
